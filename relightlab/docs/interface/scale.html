Scale

We can specify a segment on the image and assign a lenght in millimiters. This will be used for taking measurements in the viewer, once the RTI has been generated.

Click the 'Set new scale measurement...' button and click on the edges of the segment you know the lenght off, then enter the measurement in mm.

Pixel size and image with and height will be computed.
